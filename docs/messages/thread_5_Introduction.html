I'm with you about encouraging more boxers. I think there is much
concern about this with some that have been boxing for quite a while
and their concerns are valid.

However, I've often noticed that they are boxers in areas with many,
many boxers and many, many boxes.
Here in the midwest we're just excited to get to find anyone elses boxes.

I think those are both excellent arenas to teach responsible boxing.
One thing to keep in mind is not to refer to anyone else's boxes
specifically unless they've given you permission. This seems to
bother some people and they are their creative property.

Have fun and good luck. I would also agree that after you've boxed
for a few more months and found a variety of box types you'll be in a
better position to help others learn.
