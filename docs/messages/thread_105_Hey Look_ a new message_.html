We haven't had a message here in a looong time. How is everyone out
there? With the weather changing, will that increase or decrease your
boxing?

Over on atlasquest, one of those profile questions is something along
the lines of "what is the most unusual thing you've discovered while
letterboxing" to which I have to say, "that I love boxing in cold
weather!"

I'm preparing boxes and plan on planting soon and forthwith throughout
Kentucky and SW Indiana.

Kentucky isn't really a big boxing state, but we're doing a statewide
initiative of planting Superheroes around the state. If any of you
would like to throw a stamp in for the mix, I'd be glad to plant for
you. I'll soon be putting out the scene from Jean Gray and Cyclops'
last battle. It ran as a postal, wherein the comic cover changed to
have them saying "It's all about the hike!" while the other is
saying "F Count! F Count." Just a little tongue in cheek.

I'm hoping to revisit patches soon, as well. Real life craziness is
finally slowing down, enabling (I hope) lots of December boxing. For a
change, Teepsman and I are staying at home for Christmas. After 20
years, that's something new. The tree is up, but needs ornaments
added. That's a start. Last year, it was the 18th when we finally
thought about decorations and added a wreath to the door, but by that
time, we were about to head to DisneyWorld for Christmas, so there was
no tree. Both of us thought we were living an after-school special.

Happy late Thanksgiving to you all - I hope by now that you guys are
finally getting hungry and not feeling so full from eating too much
turkey and trimmings.
