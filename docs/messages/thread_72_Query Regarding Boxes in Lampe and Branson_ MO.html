Query Regarding Boxes in Lampe and Branson, MO

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-14 06:32:35 UTC-07:00
Good Morning!
 
We have a boxer here in Texas, TexasLuvBug, that has left the game and left behind quite a mess for us - all but two of her boxes are gone and yet we are unable to modify her clues, so we have folks who go looking for her boxes and are frustrated to come up empty-handed, and they are that much more frustrated when they learn that it is a known problem.
 
She has two boxes in MO - Legend of the Dogwood in Lampe and B is for Branson in Branson.  Can anyone tell me the status of those two boxes?
 
I am going to the Webmasters about adopting the ones here in Texas so I can retire the ones that are gone and save folks the trouble of looking.  I am also willing to adopt the ones in MO, except that I am unable to do any maintenance on them.  SO - assuming those boxes are still in place, is there anyone who might want to physically adopt the boxes and the clues?  If so, I'll be glad to incorporate their request into mine when I email the Webmasters.  If no one there is able to adopt them, I'll adopt the clues so that at the very least, they can be updated as reports come in about the boxes.
 
Her AQ listings pose a bit of a problem thus far, since she last logged in three days before her one year was up and her boxes would have been up for adoption.  But that's another problem for another day.
 
If anyone cares for me to go into detail about my certainty that she is out of the game, I can certainly do that, but I figured that for the sake of brevity, I would not include that information right now.  Suffice it to say - I know she's gone.
 
So what do y'all think?
 
BarefootLucy

Dallas Boxes for rozebud (was Re: [LetterboxingMidwest] Re: Query Regarding Boxes in Lampe and Branson, MO)

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-20 08:08:28 UTC-07:00
Hey Rozebud,
 
I haven't been to Dallas to look for boxes in a couple of years, but I look at the listing of planters on LBNA and aside from the single box guys who I don't know, there isn't a bad planted in the bunch!
 
Celtic Lions has especially clever placements, but a number of their boxes are missing or at risk because they have been unable to maintain them lately.  They are pretty good about keeping their listings up to date though. 
 
Mater et regisfilia supposedly has excellent boxes, but figuring them out is a challenge. 
 
Viewfinder and Puddle Splasher are sisters who have a knack for finding cool places and who do good solid boxes with nice stamps. 
 
Sprite and Highlander, Blue Butterfly, Silver Eagle, Baby Bear, JB Kokopelli, wandaandpete, Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter are all out-of-towners who are well-established box placers in their home areas and they all do excellent boxes. 
 
I think if I were going to go boxing up there, I would look toward Irving.  There are a lot of boxes in that area that I have my eye on, if I ever get that way again. 
 
Lucy

Well Irving is more to the Tarrant County side of Dallas.  Tarrant County is more Fort Worth-ish.  I would look toward the area between Dallas and Fort Worth.  That seems to be where the most activity is and the best boxes, as far as I can tell.  They recently had a gather at Grapevine Lake that sounded like a lot of fun, even if I missed it - lol.  Look for all the Moose boxes.  Those were the result of that gathering.
