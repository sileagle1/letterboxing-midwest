Hi, I'm Roze; 'trail name' is rozebud. Just started LBing in March,
after a friend (trail name geckospot) from Delaware (where I'm from
originally) visited and infected me with the madness. We went for
all the St. Louis area boxes we could ID at that time, and found 7
of 11 over a 4-day span.

About a month later, we were in DC for a party, and I got my first
official stamps - the "Kid Gloves" series in Rock Creek Park. Found
two of the three, and also a micro-box added by another boxer.

I've now been first finder of a few new boxes in the area (check out
the Queeny Park series by Fairy Princess Fiona - very nice stamps!)
and I'm kinda looking for some other folks in St. Louis interested
in this hobby. I hope to plant a few new boxes myself this summer,
once the demands of my job slow up a bit after the end of the month.

Greetings all, and hey - anyone in the Lou' please holla in my
general direction. Thanks!

Hi all,

We aren't new members, but I have been lurking for a while and
thought I should just come out in the open. We started letterboxing
last October here in the Kansas City area and have become pretty
obsessive about it. There are quite a few boxes in the area, but it
doesn't seem like there are that many real active boxers. We have
been trying to help, we've planted 7 in KC so far and have plans for
more. So if you're in the area...why not box? Anyway, we hope to
get out around the midwest a little more this summer, I'm originally
from Nebraska, so I appreciate how beautiful the midwest can be, but
my wife is from Maine, so for her, if there's no ocean view it's not
scenic. I'll get her to love the wide open spaces yet.

See ya on the trail,
