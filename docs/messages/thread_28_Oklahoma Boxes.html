A few new boxes went out in Oklahoma yesterday. My mom and I
discovered a great new place in Norman. It's the "George M Sutton
Urban Wilderness Area" It's just south of Rock Creek Road on Sooner
Road (12th street in Norman). We've just explored a bit but it seems
really great. Woodlands, Lakes, Fields and even a small cemetary.

For those of you in OK check it out. It's also barely maintained.
Which means that boxes shouldn't be discovered by maintenance crews.
