Hope to plant!

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-07-18 01:35:20 UTC
My bags are not (yet) packed, but I'll be in St. Louis for a few days
starting tomorrow.

I am hoping to plant 2-3. I have two: Meet Me in St. Louis &
Clydesdale completely ready to go (I like to carve; hate assembling
boxes). If I have time, I might carve a micro as well. I would
really like to place a UCity Lion box, as that is my home town.

I'm not sure how much time there'll be for finding. My kids get
awfully whiny if the temperature is much above 90 and I know how humid
StL in the summer can be!

And, my main purpose for going is to see the Chihuly exhibit at the
Botanical Gardens and to see some chums I haven't seen in a while.

DB

Well, that was a bust. If anyone's seen the news, let me tell you-

In the course of my work, I talk to general
contractors all over the country, and yesterday I
happened to talk to several from St. Louis. I've
watched the news, but until I talked to them, and now
reading what dewberry wrote, I really had no picture
of how bad things are there.

I hope y'all in that area know that you can count on
the rest of us to help out in any way we can. And I
know it seems bad now, and IS bad, but you will
persevere and move forward. It's happening across the
Gulf Coast and it will happen in Saint Louis.

Good luck and let us know what we can do to help you.

BarefootLucy
(Texas)
