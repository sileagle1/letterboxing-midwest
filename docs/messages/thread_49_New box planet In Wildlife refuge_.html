From: windfire_witch (windfire_witch@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-10-03 22:21:56 UTC
The refuge has a great camp site, Camp Doris. It is for tents or RV
camping. They have from Primative site to elecric site. You can get
an electrical site even if you are in a tent if they have any open.
Camp Doris also has one set of bath houses on the grounds with
running water. It has toilets, sinks and showers. The showers are
preset tempatures and sometimes the hot water runs out quick. Many
nights I have ended up with cold showers. They have a check in
station when you enter the site and you can buy ice and some sodas at
the station. No store on the refuge though. Camp Doris is set along
side of the Lake and is named after a miners camp that was there in
the early 1900's. From the camp ground you can hike to the education
center and on alot of nature trails. My family and I live in Lawton
which is only about 20 minutes from the refuge. We love camping out
at Doris. We are at the refuge almost every weekend hiking.

History of the refuge:
http://www.fws.gov/southwest/refuges/wichitamountains/refhist.html
Camp Doris Site:
http://www.fws.gov/southwest/refuges/wichitamountains/doris.html
