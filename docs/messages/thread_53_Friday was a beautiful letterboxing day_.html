Sandwiched in between some rainy, cold weather was a beautiful day
Friday! Our kids had off for parent teacher conferences, so we hit the
road and went out to explore Manhattan, Kansas. We were three for
three and had a nice time searching for prairie boxes. Besides the
boxes, we went to the insect zoo at K-State. (Not really a destination
experience, but fun.) The kids held giant hissing cockroaches and
petted the tarantula. We did plant a box on the way back, so if you're
on an I-70 road trip you'll have to check out Camp Daisy (clues not
posted yet, but soon.)
