Favorite Vacation Box

From: ks_letterboxers (ks_letterboxers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-26 04:00:05 UTC
I've been lurking around without posting for awhile and thought I
should go ahead and start posting as there looks like we're getting a
nice group of boxing midwesterners.

My family of 3 sons and one dh box on the Kansas side of the Kansas
City area, but we love finding and placing boxes wherever we go. We
just returned from vacation through Iowa, Minnesota and Wisconsin and
just over the boarder of Michigan. We placed a new box in each state
(clues to come later) and had a great time finding 10 boxes plus 1 HH.

Our favorite find came when we took our minivan up what ended up being
four wheeler trails to a fire tower in Wisconsin. We chugged up some
mighty sandy roads, but after finally reaching the top we found the
box and real wild blueberries covering the hillside to boot! The kids
have not stopped talking about wanting to go back to that letterbox.

Allison
(Kim's sister-in-law)
