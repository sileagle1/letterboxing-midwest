OK/AR Trip

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2006-08-07 00:15:23 UTC
I just returned from vacation to Arkansas via southeast Oklahoma and
it was great! Thanks to letterboxes placed in Beavers Bend Resort
Park in OK, my family stayed there and thoroughly enjoyed ourselves.
Another example of letterboxing introducing me to a place I never
heard of before. Another pleasant surprise was Mt. Magazine in AR.
There is a new State Park up there now and a beautiful Lodge that
just opened in May. We had a third floor room with a balcony
overlooking the valley below and it was incredible. Lots for the
kids to do, too.

Other places that were equally enjoyable were the Talimena Scenic
Byway, Mt. Nebo, Altus wineries, Petit Jean SP and De Gray SP.
Though I only found the boxes in OK (none were found in AR where we
went), I placed 2 in OK and 3 in AR for future travelers. Since LBNA
is down, I will include the links to these 5 new boxes here in case
anyone wants to get started right away. I highly recommend going to
these places.

Beavers Bend: http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/OK/Beaversbend/

Talimena Byway - OK:
http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/OK/TalimenaOK/

Talimena Byway - AR:
http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/AR/TalimenaAR/

AR High: http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/AR/ARHigh/

Petit Polecat: http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/AR/PPolecat/

Have fun,
Silver Eagle

Hey Kim, good to hear from you again. I found both of your boxes:
Italian Festival & Family Fun. I found Hit The Road Jack HH in
Family Fun and brought it back to TX. You must have taken it during
our Event last March. I was the only one to stamp into that box and
it was a long hike since I unknowingly chose the long way to it.
Nice hike and box.

I also found Playin Poohsticks, Red Ranger, and 3 of the 4 Broken
Bow Reservoir letterboxes. The lake was down and I could not
determine which tree was supposed to be in the water. Thanks for
bringing me to this place and providing boxes.
