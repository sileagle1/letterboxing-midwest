Greetings,
The Indiana DNR, in collaboration with local geocachers, has revised the recent 
geocaching policy for caches on DNR owned and managed properties.  This policy 
also applies for letterboxing activities.  Most changes to the policy benefit 
geocachers, but one aspect shared among the two policies is the licensing of 
physical geocaches on DNR lands.  
 
The Indiana DNR is currently working with the various geocaching websites to 
remove all geocache listings online pending a new license is submitted.  Similar 
to the other sites, the Indiana DNR is seeking the archival of DNR land 
geocaches and letterboxes currently.  
 
The full text of the policy and licensing process can be found on one of our 
newer web pages at www.geocaching.dnr.in.gov.
 
If there are any questions, please do not hesitate.  I will be overseeing the 
permitting statewide through the end of the year and will be able to provide 
assistance in identifying caches in need of the new license.  
 
Thanks
