Post Mid-MO Gathering Report

From: lnd_crzr (rmed_mountain@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-05-14 04:42:05 UTC
A grand time was had by all! This was our third annual Mid-MO Spring
LB Gathering. The theme was pirates. The location was Graham cave
State park. Many camped Friday, but things didn't officialy kick-off
until Saturday morning. We had right at 50 people show up from the Mid-
MO LB community and also two groups three groups from the KC area and
four from the St Louis area. The weather was perfect and the
waterfalls were flowing. About half camped Saturday night as we
feasted on campfire roasted Fajitas and enjoyed wonderful
entertainment by Fox-Fyr and e-volution.

For more details, pictures and info on past and future events, take a
peek at the Mid-MO Blog at www.midmoletterboxing.blogspot.com

Enjoy!

Lnd-Crzr
