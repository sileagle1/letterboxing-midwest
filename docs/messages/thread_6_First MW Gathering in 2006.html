First MW Gathering in 2006

From: Liz (Liz@morewisdom.com) | Date: 2005-12-18 11:51:44 UTC-05:00
 Hi Everyone,
 
The first gathering happening in the MidWest will be the Winter BBQ in Louisville, KY on January 21st.   We'll be meeting up at noon, searching out boxes in Louisville while playing a different form of Letterboxing Bingo than the GL group, then capping off the afternoon with a little bit of BBQ in the form of stickless shish kabobs, deviled eggs, potato salad and such.  Bring a dish, bring some cooties, and bring on the fun!  Speedsquare is already planning on bringing a special KY based treat in honor of the locale.
 
A special thank you shout out to people that have generously offered to donate stamps for planting here in Louisville either permanently or temporarily, including Bootzoocrew, drgdlg, DS, Kellybean, Ramdelt, She Who Plants, Sits N Knits, and TP.
 
For more details of the event, please check at AtlasQuest, under the "Toolbox" option.  Or if I can answer any questions, please ask away!
 
MoWizLiz
