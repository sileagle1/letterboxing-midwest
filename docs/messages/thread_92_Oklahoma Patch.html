From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2007-06-28 16:04:38 UTC
Well, I think this group is smaller than most others, so that can
affect things. Probably less people = less ideas, but should also
make it easier to make a decision. I thought there were a fair
number of MO boxers - aren't any/many of them on this board?

In the Great Lakes region we already had a "logo" associated with our
Yahoo message board. (It had been designed by SpringChick). I just
asked her if we could use it for a patch design, After she agreed I
posted a message to the group to see how much interest there would be
in patches with this design and got a good response. I sold 200
patches in less than a year and then reordered another 100 recently
with our new design (which includes Minnesota).

I think the Southeast group did something similar to us, just used a
logo that was already established for the group. I think Mid-
Atlantic and Pacific Northwest posted ideas and then used the poll
feature to tally votes. I don't remember how long the process took,
but probably a month or two.

I like the idea of REGIONAL patches, since I sure don't have room on
my pack to put a patch for every state! We always purchase a patch
(if available) when we box in a specific region.

So far I like the "corn & wheat" or the "barn" designs the best. I'd
probably prefer the "Letterboxing Midwest" text at top and bottom,
rather than listing all the states. This concept (representative
image w/o states) would be similar to the PNW design (Slug) and
MidAtlantic (sun/star/compass?).

Since we've already boxed in Iowa, we'll definitely be interested in
purchasing a patch.

Wisconsin Hiker
