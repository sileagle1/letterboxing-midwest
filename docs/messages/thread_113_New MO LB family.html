That's what we did on our first find - there were some people walking
by on the path - but we took the box out of the way and I was logging
in while my boys were hunting for acorns and bugs and sticks. So, I'm
sure it looked like normal boys playing in the woods. We made sure to
retrieve and re-hide while no one was around.

We usually take nature journals and field guides on a hike anyway - so
that will be a good cover, I hope, for the nature-related boxes.
Before discovering LB, we would often find a place to sit away from
the main trail where the boys could write or draw something in their
journals that they had just discovered, or to try to identify a tree
with our field guides - so incorporating the logging of the box into
that activity will be easy, I hope. I don't imagine we will try an
urban box with the boys for awhile. Maybe save those for times when
my husband and I are getting away for an afternoon or evening.
