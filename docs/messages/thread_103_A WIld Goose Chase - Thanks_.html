A WIld Goose Chase - Thanks!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2007-10-08 03:52:16 UTC
I just wanted to say that we really enjoyed attending our
first "Midwest Region" event. Big Purple Foot & ScoutAngel (and
probably other assistants) did a nice job of organizing the event.
The food was delicious - lots of tasty salads and someone even
brought baked beans just like my mom makes - YUM!

We spent the first several hours eating and exchanging, since there
were many boxers attending that we hadn't seen at previous events.
Of course we also enjoyed catching up with old friends from the Great
Lakes region!

Eventually it was time to hit the trails to find some boxes, which
were nicely scattered throughput the park. Who would have thought it
would be quite SO HOT in October?!?! It was 88 on Saturday and 89
today, so we got a workout in the heat. However we found lots of
nice boxes as a reward, and were thankful that there weren't many
bugs at all.

I've posted a group photo from the event in the Photos section of
this message board.

We're looking forward to returning to the Quad Cities area again
since there are many more boxes to find and it is a fairly easy drive
from the Milwaukee, WI area.

Thanks to everyone who contributed to a nice event!

Wisconsin Hiker & Martini Man
