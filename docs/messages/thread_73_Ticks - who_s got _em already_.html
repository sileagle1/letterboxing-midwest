Anyone made friends with the ticks yet this year?

After planting two in SW Indiana on Sunday, we had three, thankfully
just hanging out on clothing. The third one I discovered in my hair on
Monday, after being on the trail Sunday. I wear my hair up on the
trails to keep from giving them a foothold onto the old bod. I kind of
think that one was in the car from the day before though, seeing how it
was entangled and not being a sucker tick.

Blech. Nasty things they.
