From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-14 17:54:13 UTC-07:00
My husband isn't that crazy about it either, but he
goes because he likes hiking and he likes hanging out
with us.

The inimitable Ruby Tuesday of Dallas coined a phrase
for spouses who aren't really letterboxers, but who go
along and don't complain and are even quite helpful
sometimes - if they are good-spirited about
letterboxing in spite of not being "into it". She
called hers her LSS - Long-Suffering Spouse. It's a
term of endearment!

Lucy
