From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-12-14 00:55:24 UTC
Glad to see this Group established and I'm happy to be
a member, even though I don't live in any of the
states. I am from TX and have been letterboxing for
over 3 years, but I like to belong to regional groups
near TX and ones with states that I have boxes in, and
both apply to this group. Hopefully this Group will
help letterboxing grow in the Heartland like it has in TX.
We started the TX Talklist about 2 years ago when there
were less than 10 people that I knew of participating in
the whole state. Soon, more and more people started joining
the group, discussing ideas and encouraging each other in
ways impossible to do on the Big List. Now we have over
150 members and are about to have our third Event. Which
leads me to my invite, especially to those of you in Oklahoma
or Arkansas since you are closer. The TX Boxcar LB Event
will be held on March 18, 2006 at Rusk State Park in Northeast
TX. Please visit the website for more details:

http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/Events/TBLE/

I probably won't be very active in the List, but will enjoy
watching it grow over the coming months. Have fun and don't
be afraid to speak up.

Silver Eagle
