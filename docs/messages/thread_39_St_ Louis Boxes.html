St. Louis Boxes

From: CompassPoints (ltrboxingrichters@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-06 04:59:03 UTC
We were able to enjoy finding Fairy Princess Fiona's boxes in Queeny
Park this week. Her stamps were wonderful and that park is a
wonderful place.

My husband, son 11 and daughter 7 spent part of a morning and an
afternoon hunting. The kids gave out at the end and my husband stayed
with them but we managed to find all 7 plus the 8th with help from the
placer.

We also found one by Princess Lea in Wood River, IL. One alluded us
but the other was great and I highly recommend this Lewis & Clark site
to anyone remotely interested in their exploration.

On the way home we found a box on Interstate 44 at highway marker 52.
I can't remember all the details and the web site is down so I can't
tell you the placer, even though we've found his before. Sorry! But
it was fun and an easy one for my kids to enjoy. Missouri rest areas
are amazing.

Not bad for a trip when most of the week there was over 100 degrees
and you sweated just laying still.
