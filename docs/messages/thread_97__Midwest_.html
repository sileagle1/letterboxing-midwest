From: coreyschurman (coreyschurman@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-20 14:16:34 UTC
Growing up in Nebraska and now living in Missouri, I figure the
Midwest as kind of a line from Chicago to St. Louis, across to KC and
then down to Dallas and Houston. Then west side is up the eastern
foothills of the Rockies. I would break that area down into three
sub-areas: Great Lakes, Northern Plains and Southern Plains.

I lived in Cincinnati for seven years and they always called it the
Midwest, but that's an east coast construct, people in Cincinnati are
way more east coast (read not as friendly) than here in KC.

Just my 2 cents.

Craw
