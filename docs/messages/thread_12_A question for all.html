A question for all

From: jojoma22 (jojoma22@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-02-10 05:15:20 UTC
In the yahoo group for new letterboxers the question was asked, "Which
website do you use the most, LbNA or Altas Quest?" I wasn't familiar
with AQ, so I checked it out. I live in Iowa and AQ listed only SEVEN
letterboxes in my entire state. SEVEN! I'm glad I use LbNA because it
shows a whole lot more. I'd be really depressed it I didn't know about
LbNA. I'm not saying AQ is bad, it just seems it's not caught on in
the midwest. So, you midwestern letterboxers out there...What website
do you use the most?

Goose Chaser

From: CompassPoints (ltrboxingrichters@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-02-11 17:48:21 UTC
When Atlas quest first came out I was excited about it's variety of
logging options. It was fun to see my finds listed.

I listed all of mine and my parents in OK however I still used LBNA
to host the clues.

I always check AQ but messages, updates etc. However, for clues my
first check is LBNA. Especially since they've added some of those
same options.

I do however, really like the "mapquest" type option on AQ to help me
find boxes on a certain route.
