Howdy from a 1 week old newbie from St. Louis!  My kids and I (7 & 4) carved our stamps last Saturday and went to Tower Grove Park.  I thought we could find several boxes the first time, and my kids would love it.  But we found the Turkish Pavilion one first, and then the kids saw the playground, and it was all over.  :0)   We spent the day playing and when we were finished, were really too beat to look for anything else.  However, we did drive around a little so that we'd know where to go next time.  There were about 4 wedding parties doing pictures so I guess no way to be discrete on a beautiful Saturday.  But then again, is there any way to be discrete with a 4 year old?!
 
We plan to head to Wildwood today; I have the clues for 1 in Babler and another off 109.  Hopefully I can keep the kids interested...
